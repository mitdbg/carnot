## Privacy Statement
1. Our Commitment
We value your privacy. We operate under two core principles:

We will not sell your data to third parties.

Minimal Collection: We only capture data that is strictly necessary for our research and system evaluation.

2. Data Collection (Non-Enterprise Users)
For non-enterprise users, we collect the following information to facilitate our study on Deep Research effectiveness:
- Input Data: Documents and datasets uploaded to the Service.
- Interaction Data: The text of messages sent to the agent and the resulting agent responses.
- Metadata: Timestamps, preference signals, and basic usage statistics.

3. Use of Data
Collected data is used exclusively to:
- Analyze the performance of various Deep Research techniques.
- Improve the accuracy and efficiency of the Carnot system.
- Publish academic findings (all published data will be aggregated or anonymized to protect individual identity).

4. Data Retention
We retain research data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the research objectives or as required by institutional data retention policies.
